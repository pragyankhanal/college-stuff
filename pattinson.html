The Batman

"They think I'm hiding in the shadows, I am the shadows"

Matt Reeves didn't make this "a plain regular superhero movie", it is detective movie as well.
As an early vigilante, Batman sees to save the city from a new villian, Riddler.
He makes a lot of mistakes and fails miserably throughout the movie.
Reeves has displayed Gotham as a corrupt and crime filled city which is why it is so interesting.
The cinematography and soundtrack really gives the viewer an occasional goosebump.
