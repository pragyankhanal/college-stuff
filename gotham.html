Gotham

"One hundred thousand and ONE dollar"

This is a TV series or a Netflix show mainly about a very young Bruce Wayne and Detective Jim Gordon.
In this show Jim Gordon is seen working on cases on various Batman villians like riddler and penguin.
While Bruce Wayne is being taught about combat and ways to defend himself. He is taught about all of that
from his family butler Alfred Pennyworth who becomes Bruce's father figure after the death of his parents.
This show is of 5 seasons and Batman makes a small appereance at the end of the show.
As of now the series has come to an end and has a really good reputation among the fans.
