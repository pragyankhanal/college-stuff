Dark Knight Trilogy

"Why so serious"

The "Dark Knight" trilogy isn't just the best superhero trilogy but the best trilogy ever.
Cristopher Nolan is the man who is responsible for this masterpiece. He is known for great cinematography,
and he shows it off in this trilogy too. Cristian Bale is so flawless as both Bruce Wayne and Batman.
The story of this trilogy is taken straight out of the comics into the big screen. There are all sorts of
villians like Joker, Two Face, Ra's al Ghul, Bane.
The actor who persued Joker, Heath Ledger, won an oscar for his role as well.
