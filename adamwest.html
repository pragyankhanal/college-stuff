Batman

1966-1968

This is a TV series is the first live action appereance of Batman.
In this show the two main characters are Batman and Robin portrayed by Adam West and Burt Ward.
There are numbers of villians that make the show fun like Joker and Riddler.
This is is more of a comedic approach rather than the dark and serious like today.
This show is of 2 seasons and came to an end with a really good conclusion.
